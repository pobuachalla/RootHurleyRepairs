About Us

Repairing Hurleys
with Care

RooT Hurley Repairs is run by Ruaidhrí and Tadhg — two hurlers who know exactly what players need from their gear. We provide reliable hurley repair services for players who want to get more life from their sticks.

From quick fixes to full refreshes, every hurley that comes through our door is treated properly, worked on with real craft, and returned match-ready — whether it’s for training or championship day.
